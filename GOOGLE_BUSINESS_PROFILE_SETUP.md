# Google Business Profile Setup Guide for Aziza Home Dubai

## What is Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business)?

Google Business Profile (GBP) is a free tool that helps your business appear in Google Search and Maps when people search for interior design and furnishing services in Dubai. It's crucial for local SEO.

## Benefits for Aziza Home

- **Local Search Visibility**: Appear in "near me" searches and Google Maps
- **Rich Snippets**: Show business hours, reviews, photos, and contact info directly in search results
- **Customer Engagement**: Respond to reviews, answer questions, post updates
- **Insights**: Track how customers find and interact with your listing
- **Trust Building**: Verified business badge increases credibility

## Step-by-Step Setup Process

### 1. Create Your Google Business Profile

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Click "Manage now" or "Add your business"
3. Sign in with your Google account (create one if needed)

### 2. Enter Business Information

**Business Name**: Aziza Home

**Business Category**: 
- Primary: Interior Designer
- Additional categories you can add:
  - Furniture Store
  - Home Furnishings Store
  - Interior Decoration
  - Home Staging Service

**Location**: Add your physical address in Dubai
- If you serve customers at their location (which you do), select "Yes, I serve customers at their location"
- Define your service area (Dubai and surrounding emirates)

**Phone Number**: +971 55 977 9635

**Website**: https://azizahomes.com

### 3. Verify Your Business

Google requires verification to ensure you're the actual business owner. Methods include:

- **Postcard verification**: Google mails a postcard with a verification code to your business address (most common, takes 5-14 days)
- **Phone verification**: If available for your business type
- **Email verification**: If available
- **Video verification**: For some businesses

**Important**: Don't skip verification! Your listing won't appear until verified.

### 4. Complete Your Profile (Critical for SEO)

Once verified, complete ALL sections:

#### Business Description
Write a compelling 750-character description including:
- What Aziza Home does
- Your unique value proposition
- Target keywords: "interior design Dubai", "home furnishing Dubai", "expat relocation services", "holiday home setup"
- Areas served: Dubai, UAE

#### Business Hours
- Add regular hours
- Update for holidays
- Add special hours for Ramadan/Eid

#### Services
List your key services:
- Turnkey Home Furnishing Packages
- Holiday Home Setup & Design
- Expat Relocation Services
- Investor Property Furnishing
- Custom Interior Design

Add descriptions and pricing for each service.

#### Photos (CRITICAL)
Upload high-quality photos:
- **Logo**: Your Aziza Home logo
- **Cover photo**: Hero image of your best work
- **Interior photos**: At least 10-15 photos of completed projects
- **Team photos**: Your team at work
- **Before/after**: Transformation photos
- **Videos**: Virtual tours if available

Google recommends:
- Minimum 720px wide
- JPG or PNG format
- Under 5MB

#### Attributes
Select all that apply:
- Woman-owned business
- Online estimates
- On-site services
- Free consultations (if applicable)
